# Autism Screening Referral Options — Okanagan/BC (Adults)

*Source: MHSU referral response letter. MHSU does NOT provide adult autism assessments.*

## Fee-for-Service Options

### 1. UBC Okanagan
- Training center — assessments done by graduate students under supervision
- Handles: learning disabilities, psychoeducational, neuropsychological assessments for autism
- **Cost:** ~$400–$500
- **Phone:** 250.807.8241
- **Email:** ipc.ok@ubc.ca

### 2. Autism Society of British Columbia (Autism BC)
- Okanagan pathways resource page
- **Website:** autismbc.ca (Okanagan pathways section)

### 3. Pathways Central Okanagan
- Directory of local services

### 4. Dr. Jodi Morris
- Private practice
- Youth and Family Psychology Assessment Services

### 5. The Neurodivergent Team
- Online assessments
- Licensed in BC and Ontario

## Notes
- All options are private pay (fee-for-service) — not covered by MSP
- UBC Okanagan is the most affordable option
- The Neurodivergent Team is fully online if in-person is not feasible
